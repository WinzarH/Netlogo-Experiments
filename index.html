Netlogo experiments

I created this page to share with colleagues some of my work on Complexity Theory using Agent-based Modelling.

This started with an attempt to replicate a paper by Goldenberg et al. in 2001. Using an exact replication, using different software, I found quite different results from those reported in the original paper. It has taken me on a journey.

Goldenberg, J., Libai, B., Muller, E., 2001. “Talk of the Network: A Complex Systems Look at the Underlying Process of Word-of-Mouth,” Marketing Letters, Vol. 12 (3), pp. 211-223.

These programs

Programs here are for a working paper provisionally titled:

A Replication and Extension of Goldenberg, Libai & Muller: Demonstrating a complex systems approach to developing and testing theory
